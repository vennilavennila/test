SANFORD SUITE

TOURISTS is a hotel and riverside retreat inspired by the classic American roadside motor lodge, set on the banks of the Hoosic
River in the Berkshires city of North Adams. Our 48-room property is a union of design and nature, home to woodland trails,
riverbank vistas, sculptural installations, and more. Using common, organic materials, your room is both haven and trailhead,
connecting you with your vacation self and serving as a basecamp for adventure.
